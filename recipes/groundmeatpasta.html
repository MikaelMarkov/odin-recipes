Ground Meat and Pasta

Ground Meat and Pasta are both well known and simple recipes that when combined create a powerful source of protein and carbohidrates

Ingredients

  • From 500g to 1kg of ground meat (of your choice)
  • 500g of pasta
  • 2 peppers of your choice (yellow, green or red)
  • seasonings of your like or the ones indicated above

Steps

  1. Put the pasta in a pan with water and let it boil for around 25 minutes - or until it gets al dente for you
  2. While the pasta is being coocked, cut the 2 peppers in small pieces and reserve
  3. Put 1kg of ground meat in another pan with a little bit of olive oil
  4. Seal the meat (it must take around 5 minutes)
  5. When the meat is completely seald, add the seasonings to it, I suggest 20g of fine herbs, 5g of lemmon pepper, and 10g of cumin
  6. Mix the seasoning well and keep mixing the meat from time to time, avoiding the meat to stick to the bottom of the pan
  7. When the meat is almost finished add the peppers to it
  8. Let the meat cook until there is no more water in the bottom of the pan
  9. Dry the pasta and put it in an adequate recipent
  10. Mix the meat to the pasta and serve the dish
